Reaching Out to Other People

Meeting New People

Making new friends expands our world and helps us form new connections. It can be hard to know where to start, but there are some good tips to keep in mind! First, don't be afraid to approach someone new. You can compliment their outfit, or mention a shared interest. Don't be afraid to ask them questions--people will usually be happy to talk about themselves.

Listen to Others

An important aspect of talking to others is listening. If you only talk about yourself, or don't pay attention to peoples' answers, they'll be less likely to feel appreciated. Active listening helps with this. Keep eye contact with the person you're talking to, and take a confident stance.

Seek Out Opportunities

You have to take the first step in reaching out. Initiate conversation and share openly, to encourage others to do the same. In addition, keep looking for chances to connect with those around you. They can happen in places you might not expect, like a grocery store or a classroom.
