Activity Ideas

Finding Activities

There's a lot to do in your own community, even though it can be hard to tell. You can try volunteering, or looking for groups that are focused around things you are interested in. Events can also be a great way to get out of the apartment!/p>

Ideas to Try

  • Visit a local part
  • Eat a meal with friends--have a potluck!
  • Have a game night
  • Do an escape room
  • Have a city-wide scavenger hunt
  • Go hiking or camping
  • Play a sport
  • Visit a museum or library
  • Learn a new skill with others

To Find an Activity Group

Ask your friends to come to events with you, so you can all have a good time. If you don't know anyone there, strike up a conversation with someone new!
